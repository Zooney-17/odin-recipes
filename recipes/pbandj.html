Peanut Butter and Jelly

A quick and delicious sandwhich for a quick snack!

Similar to Cereal, this recipe is fast and easy, requiring very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place down two slices of bread on a plate for flat surface.
  2. Use a butterknife to scoop out and apply peanut butter to one slice of bread.
  3. Repeat the previous step for Jelly for the unused slice of bread.
  4. Combine both slices of bread via the used side.
  5. (Optional) Slice the sandwhich in half with preferred configuration
