Cereal

A quick and easy meal for every occasion. Cereal never fails!

I'm gonna give it to you straight. This is a very very VERY easy recipe. There isn't alot of steps needed so sit back and enjoy the process.

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Milk
  • Choice of cereal (Cheerios in our example)

Steps

  1. Place bowl down on a flat stable surface.
  2. Add cereal into the bowl. Make sure to leave space for the milk.
  3. Pour milk onto the cereal ensuring nothing spills.
  4. Enjoy!
